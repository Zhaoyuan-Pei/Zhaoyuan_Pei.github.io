既往项目 Projects

竹简文字识别项目 Bamboo Slip Text Recognition Project

项目负责人。构建数字人文工作流程，将竹简文献图像转化为机器可识别文本；通过灰度化、二值化处理及人工智能辅助文字识别技术，提高转录准确率；成果在北京大学数字人文研究中心成立典礼上展示。
Project Lead. Developed a digital humanities workflow to convert images of bamboo-slip manuscripts into machine-readable text; applied grayscale transformation, binarization techniques, and AI-assisted character recognition to improve transcription accuracy; showcased at the inauguration of the Research Center for Digital Humanities at Peking University.

毛泽东时代非正式金融的档案研究 Archival Research on Informal Finance in Maoist China

基于对中国多地省、市、县级档案馆的系统调查而展开的持续性研究项目，探讨毛泽东时期（1949–1978）高利贷及非正式金融的制度演变，重点分析位置挪用机制、干部参与形式以及国家治理失效的结构性原因。
Ongoing research project based on extensive archival investigation across provincial, municipal, and county-level archives in China; examines the institutional evolution of usury and informal finance under Maoist governance (1949–1978), focusing on mechanisms of positional appropriation, cadre involvement, and structural causes of regulatory failure.
