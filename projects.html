Projects

Bamboo Slip Text Recognition Project

Project Lead. Developed a digital humanities workflow to convert images of bamboo-slip manuscripts into machine-readable text. The project applied grayscale transformation, binarization techniques, and AI-assisted character recognition to improve transcription accuracy. The project was showcased at the inauguration of the Research Center for Digital Humanities at Peking University.

Archival Research on Informal Finance in Maoist China

Ongoing research project based on an extensive archival investigation across provincial, municipal, and county-level archives in China. The project examines the institutional evolution of usury under Maoist governance (1949–1978), focusing on mechanisms of positional appropriation, cadre involvement, and state regulatory failure.
